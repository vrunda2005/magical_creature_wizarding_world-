Magical Creatures in Hogwards

Beast

Beasts: This category includes magical creatures that are typically large, dangerous, and often have violent tendencies. Dragons, Acromantulas (giant spiders), and Manticores fall under this category.

Being

Beings: Beings refer to creatures that possess intelligence and are capable of human-like communication. This category includes creatures like Centaurs, House-Elves, and Veela. Muggles wizards and witches Goblins, vampires, hags, giants, house-elves, veela, and werewolves in their human form were also given this status.

Spirit

Spirits: These are non-corporeal entities that often inhabit specific locations or objects. Examples include Ghosts (such as Nearly Headless Nick), Poltergeists (like Peeves), and Boggarts.

Fantastic Beasts

This category comprises a wide range of magical creatures, both small and large, with extraordinary qualities. It includes creatures like Hippogriffs, Nifflers, Bowtruckles, and Thestrals.

Dark Creatures

Dark creatures are often associated with dark magic or malevolence. They include Dementors, Inferi (reanimated corpses controlled by dark wizards), and Werewolves.

Water Creatures

These are magical creatures that dwell primarily in aquatic environments. Merpeople, Grindylows, and Kelpies are examples of water creatures.

Domesticated Creatures

Domesticated creatures are magical animals that are often kept as pets or companions by wizards and witches. This category includes creatures like Owls, Cats (such as Crookshanks), and Rats (like Scabbers).

Plant Creatures

These are magical creatures that are either partially or entirely made up of plant matter. The Whomping Willow and Venomous Tentacula are examples of plant creatures.
