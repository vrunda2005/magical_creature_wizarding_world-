What is Wizarding World?

The Wizarding World takes place in a parallel magical civilization that coexists with the non-magical (Muggle) world but is concealed from the general public. It is a magical world where witches and wizards learn magic at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry, magical animals wander, and a sophisticated network of magical organizations and societies exists. It includes all of the magical components, locations, characters, and events that makeup Harry Potter's universe.

The following are important aspects of the Wizarding World:

1. Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry:Harry Potter and the School of Witchcraft and Wizardry: The well-known school where young witches and wizards receive magical training and learn about the magical world.
2. Magical Creatures: House elves, goblins, centaurs, dragons, hippogriffs, and other magical creatures can be found in the Wizarding World.
3. Magic Communities: Diagon Alley and Hogsmeade are hidden wizarding neighborhoods and villages where magical persons may buy goods and socialise.
4. Ministry of Magic: The British government's governing body for magical matters, responsible for keeping order and regulating magical practises.
5. Quidditch: A broomstick-based wizarding sport.

Dark Arts and Dark Wizards: evil magic and evil wizards like Lord Voldemort pose substantial risks to the magical community in the Wizarding World.
